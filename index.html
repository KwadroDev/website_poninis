Story


DreamLands, is the world where the Ponini's live, a place full of adventure and fantasy.
The parallel universe that we can all access today. Animals, nature and almost everything around them is the dominant species. Within the DreamsVerse we are located in HorseKingdon waging a battle against an evil force that has corrupted some Ponini's, the strongest fight to restore peace to their kingdom and protect the magic of the Mythical beings. Over 10,000 wonderful Ponini's await you, ready to become part of your collection.
